About Me

My name is Mauricio Vargas Sepúlveda. I am a Chilean and I studied as an undergraduate in Chile. My name is too complicated in many languages and my nickname, Pachá (帕夏), is preferred in almost any country including mine.
I am currently finishing my Professional Degree of Business Administration at Faculty of Economics and Business, University of Chile (in Chile this involves an extra year after the bachelor’s degree – somewhat similar to an applied master’s degree) along with a Trade Policy Minor and I expect to graduate in December 2015. My Bachelor was in Economics and Business Administration, but I also took some courses at the Faculty of Physical and Mathematical Sciences and at the Institute for International Studies. My background includes courses ranged from Marketing, Human Resources, Optimization and Convex Analysis, Econometrics to Intellectual Property.
My thesis advisor is Professor Edgar E. Kausel and under his supervission I'm working on Structural Equation Modelling (SEM) and Education.
